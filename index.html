Our Activity

Industrial trainings, Mock Interviews

We started our journey in 2018 by teaching just a single student and now have added more than 500 students to our family and the count is increasing year by year. We are tasting success because we have solid support from our students and blessings from their parents. The sole purpose of starting this journey is to build confident,wise and hard working students who can cope up with the working standards of IT industries. We nurture technical skills to build a solid career and transform their dreams into reality. We develop the skills in students which are required by the IT Industries to face any challenges that come in their career. Therefore we conduct activities other than our regular batches are mentioned below.
  • Resume Building
  • Career Guidance
  • Mock Interviews
  • Training on: How to work in IT Industry by industry experts
  • Guest lectures on various domains to give more exposure to students to choose their path.
    • We are also conducting ONLINE batches to reach upto maximum students and guide them to shape their career.
